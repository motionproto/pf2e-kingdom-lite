Text Usage Examples

Content Hierarchy

Primary text is used for main content that needs maximum readability.

Secondary text provides supporting information without competing for attention.

Tertiary text is for metadata, timestamps, and other supplementary details.

Disabled text indicates inactive or unavailable options.

Accent text draws attention to important highlights or interactive elements.
